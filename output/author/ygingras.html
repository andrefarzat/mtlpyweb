Montréal-Python January 5-à-7

Wed 12 January 2011
By Ygingras

In Montréal Python.

Translations: fr

Our original plans for January were to have the meeting on the 17th but booking a room in a university at the beginning of the semester is a non-trivial task, not quite unlike renewing an expired passport at Complex Guy-Favreau. Facing the grim prospect of not drinking beer with Pythonistas, we decided to invite you all for a very informal 5-à-7 on 2011-01-24 at Cheval Blanc. See you there.
