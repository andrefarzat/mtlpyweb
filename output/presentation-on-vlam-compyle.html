Presentation on VLAM Compyle

Wed 04 March 2009
By Ygingras

In Presentations.

Marc-Antoine Desroches will be giving a presentation on VLAM Compyle, a just-in-time compiler for Python at the Montreal Scheme/Lisp User Group meeting next Thursday (2009-03-12) . Marc-Antoine was one of our flash presenters at Montreal Python 5. The MSLUG meeting is at 7pm in Room 3195, André-Aisenstadt Building, Université de Montréal, 2920 chemin de la Tour.

– Yannick Gingras
