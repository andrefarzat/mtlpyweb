Follow-up on the 4th Django Sprint

Tue 04 May 2010
By Ygingras

In Django, Montréal Python, Sprints.

Translations: fr

The fourth Django translation sprint was very productive. The group is now experienced and we can at last spend more time strugling with the language than with the tools. Anyone knows a good translation for "file handler"? We hope to launch a new sprint a few weeks from now; watch the blog or the mailing list for the announcement. Thanks to AUF for the food and the drinks and thanks to Brasseurs Numériques for hosting the event. Congratulation to all the sprinters for their good work:
