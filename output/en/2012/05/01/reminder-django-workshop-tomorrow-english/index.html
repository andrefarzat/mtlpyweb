Reminder : Django Workshop tomorrow [English]

Tue 01 May 2012
By Davin Baragiotta (davin@baragiotta.org)

In Montréal Python, Tutorials/Workshops.

Just a quick reminder : tomorrow is the Django Workshop in English at McGill. There's about 35 places left, so don't be shy and let's have a nice hands on Django together. Our subscription service (Eventbrite) was automatically close yesterday because the auto-limit of 40 places were all reserved, but in fact there's nearly 85 places (so near 35 left now)! So, sorry for the inconvenience and subscribe now if it's not already done and/or show up tomorrow!

  • Date : 2012-05-02 from 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM
  • Place : McGill, Rutherford Building, room 118, 3600 University Street
  • Subscription : http://montrealpython-workshop-20120502.eventbrite.ca/

See you! N.B.: exact place for the same workshop in French next week will be soon announce here.
