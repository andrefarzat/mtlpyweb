Packaging sprint #10

Step out of your buttermilk-induced coma, distutils2 needs you! We are organizing a new packaging sprint on wednesday 2010-12-08, at Google's office (1253 McGill College, suite 250). The distutils2 project is progressing rapidly, and we must continue our effort. By the way, they released their first alpha version not too long ago. We have 20 seats available, and on top of it, Google will provide us with drinks and food. If we're lucky, we might even have some of their famous baked beans. Please write down your name in our wiki: [http://wiki.montrealpython.org/index.php/Packaging\_no.10][]
