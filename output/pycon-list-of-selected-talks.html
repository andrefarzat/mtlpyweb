PyCon: the list of selected talks is online

A day or so after ConFoo, PyCon finally unveiled its list of selected talks. With talks such as API Design: Lessons Learned by Raymond Hettinger and Advanced Network Architectures With ZeroMQ by Zed Shaw, PyCon 2011 is going to be a very inspiring event. And on the topic of building inspiration, yours truly will be giving a talk on our experience organizing Montréal-Python and how others can (and should) step up and build their local communities.
