Check it out: Django Workshop Next Week [French]

Mon 07 May 2012
By Giotta

In Montréal Python.

Translations: fr

Ta-ta-ra-ta-taaaaaaaaaaa! Pythonistas, Montréal-Python Rugby League needs your help! Here comes your confrontation with Django... a bit postponed since we absolutely wanted to hold this event in a UQAMian stadium. If bad weather occurs, we've took our dispositions : we've found a covered stadium near UQAM. So, let's stop kidding, here's what you need to know about our last workshop in French of the Winter 2012 Session : Django : monter une application web en Python

  • Date: Wednesday 16th of May, from 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM
  • Place: UQAM (hourra!), Sherbrooke Building, 200 Sherbrooke West, room SH-2420

  • Subscription: on Eventbrite

  • Workshop's wiki page

  • Workshop's original post

Please, install what's needed before the workshop or show up at 5:30 PM to get help. Don't forget to subscribe even tough it's free! Be there!
