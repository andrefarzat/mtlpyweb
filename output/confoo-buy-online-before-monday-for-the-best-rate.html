ConFoo: buy online before Monday for the best rate

Thu 18 February 2010
By Ygingras

In Conferences.

Translations: fr

Sunday is officially the last day to register for ConFoo at the current price. Actually, that not quite exact because as a way to thank you for reading this blog, we offer you $100 of rebate. Simply follow this link to register at the community rate: http://confoo.ca/pymontreal You probably recall that Chris Perkins is giving a one-day training session on Web development with Python and Turbogears. You get an additional $100 of rebate if you attend to both the training and the conference. Having seen Chris in the tutorials at PyCon, I can't put enough emphasis on how good a teacher he is. This is a very unique opportunity to get high quality training on Web development with Python right here in Montréal.
