MontrealPython3 - June 26 2008 @ 6:30PM

Mon 16 June 2008
By Arach

In Presentations.

tags: meetupMontreal Pythonmontrealpython3

MontrealPython3 will be held June 26 2008 @ 6:30PM. We’ll be meeting at Standout Jobs : 3981 boul. St. Laurent, Suite 615, Montreal. Going forward, we will do one presentation per meetup. This will be more convenient for lots of people, who have to leave before the end and feel bad about it... Our presenter will be Martin Blais, who will give one of his proposed presentations (Snakefood / Beancount). PS. There will not be another huge gap between events... To make sure this is credible, we have set the next date already : July 31st. Hopefully, Hugo Boyer will be the presenter for MP4 and talk to us about "Using python to make real objects".
