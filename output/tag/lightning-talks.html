Lightning talks for MontrealPython2

Tue 18 March 2008
By Arach

In Montréal Python.

tags: lightning talksMontreal Python

As a result of brainstorming with fellow Montrealers Martin, Eric and Vadim at PyCon, we are going to introduce lightning talks in our next meetup. The idea is simple, attendees can volunteer to come up between the first and second talk during the meetup and talk about how they use of Python. Simple and informal, these lightning talks are meant to inform each other about how we use python, and eventually spark conversations. What do you think?
