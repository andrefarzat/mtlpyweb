The Benelux's Fifth Anniversary

Thu 28 April 2011
By Mlhamel

In Montréal Python.

Translations: fr

For almost 5 years, the Benelux has been delighting our taste buds and offering us amongst the best beers in Montreal. It's an opportunity for a big party, so come out this Saturday, to the Benelux, 245 Sherbrooke West, to celebrate with the team and the beer lovers.
