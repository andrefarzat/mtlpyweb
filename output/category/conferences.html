  2. Andrew Francis at EuroPython

    Mon 24 May 2010

    Andrew Francis, who did the main presentation at Montréal-Python 13, will be presenting at EuroPython. His talk on Implementing Go's Select will be a slightly shorter version of the one that he did at Montréal-Python. It's good to see fellow Montréalers presenting at large international conferences.

    Thu 11 March 2010

    Hello Pythonistas ! This Friday, at 9 am., we want to invite you to an unofficial montreal-python gathering, at Savoir-Faire Linux's booth, in the St-Pierre room (across from the blue "Dinner/Lunch" room). (Hilton Bonaventure Hotel) Proposed subjects: - Intro to Python, for newcomers - Hands-on Pylons, Django and TG2 - Distutils2, packaging ...
