Invitation to a Python gathering at Confoo

Thu 11 March 2010
By Admin

In Conferences.

Translations: fr

Hello Pythonistas ! This Friday, at 9 am., we want to invite you to an unofficial montreal-python gathering, at Savoir-Faire Linux's booth, in the St-Pierre room (across from the blue "Dinner/Lunch" room). (Hilton Bonaventure Hotel) Proposed subjects: - Intro to Python, for newcomers - Hands-on Pylons, Django and TG2 - Distutils2, packaging .deb, .rpm, .egg - Documentation in Python, Sphinx and al - ... And more than that, you can come if you're registered to Confoo after 13h30 ! Wow !
