#MTLStartupTalent Next Week

#MTLStartupTalent Next Week

Thu 27 October 2011
By Mathieu Leduc-Hamel (marrakis@gmail.com)

In Montréal Python.

Translations: fr

Next week #MTLStartupTalent will be held on monday and aims to connect companies that are hiring with talent looking for employment. Montreal-Python invite you to assist and participate and to demonstrate how dynamic and energetic are the Pythonistas of Montreal-Python. Here’s what you can expect:

  • Access to great job opportunities at great companies
  • A chance to converse with an employee or founder and start to build a relationship
  • A casual (yet serious) environment to network with others in your field of interest

If you are interested, don't forget to register your place at http://mtlstartuptalent.eventbrite.com/ See you on Thursday !
