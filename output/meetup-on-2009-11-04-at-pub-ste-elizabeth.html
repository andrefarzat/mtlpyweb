Thu 29 October 2009
By Ygingras

In Montréal Python.

Translations: fr

We'll meet at Pub Ste-Elizabeth next Wednesday (2009-11-04) at 6 PM. Don't forget about the AppEngine presentation at Station-C tonight and we still have the TurboGears sprint in the pipe for 2009-11-27.
